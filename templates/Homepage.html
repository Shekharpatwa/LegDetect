What is Genu valgum Deformity?

Genu valgum, known as knock-knees, is a knee misalignment that turns your knees inward.
When people with knock-knees stand up with their knees together, there’s a gap of 3 inches or more between their ankles. Know more



What is Bow Leg Deformity?

Bow legs (genu varum) is a condition where one or both of your child’s legs curve outward at the knees.
This creates a wider space than normal between the knees and lower legs. Know more
