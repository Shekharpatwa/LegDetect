KNEE DEFORMITY DETECTION

Knee deformity detection is web application in which knee deformity like genu valgum(knock-knees) and genu varum(bow-legs) are detected.
The deformity is detected by measuring the angle between the leg.
For this purpose, the application needs a webcam to capture the image of the person.After the image is taken application start it process and show the processed image with angle on it on the screen. And then one can note the angle and fill the form which will predict the deformity.

    • What is Genu valgum Deformity?

    Genu valgum, known as knock-knees, is a knee misalignment that turns your knees inward.
    When people with knock-knees stand up with their knees together, there’s a gap of 3 inches or more between their ankles. Know more



    What is Genu Varum Deformity?

    Bow legs (genu varum) is a condition where one or both of your child’s legs curve outward at the knees.
    This creates a wider space than normal between the knees and lower legs. Know more



    How to detect?

    The application is provided with two features

    1. Live Detection
    2. Image Detection

    Live Detection:

    In this feature the application will process the webcam continuously and will provide the continuous video on the screen. The Video streaming will only be stop by changing to other tab.
    This feature is recommended to two users where one will stand in front of webcam and other will note the angle and then fill the prediction form.


    Image Detection:

    In this feature the application will process the webcam for 5 second and then the still and processed image will appear on the screen. One can note the angle from the screen and can fill in the form to predict the deformity. This feature is recommended to single user who can stand still for 5 sec in front of the webcam and then the processed image will appear and then note the angle and fill the form.
